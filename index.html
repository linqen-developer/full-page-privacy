Privacy Policy for Full Page Screenshot Extension

Last updated: May 13, 2025

Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the Full Page Screenshot Extension (referred to as "we", "our", or "the extension") handles information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Don't Collect

Our extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We operate with a strict local-only policy, meaning all operations occur exclusively on your device.

How We Use Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • activeTab - Allows the extension to interact only with the currently active tab when you click the extension button.
  • downloads - Enables saving your screenshots directly to your device.
  • debugger - Helps ensure accurate capturing of complex page elements.
  • tabs - Provides information about the tab being captured.
  • scripting - Allows the extension to analyze page structure for perfect captures.
  • host permissions - Access to web content for seamless screenshot creation.

These permissions are used exclusively for the purpose of capturing and saving screenshots. We do not use these permissions to collect data or monitor your browsing activity.

Data Storage

All screenshots captured by our extension are saved directly to your device. We do not store copies of your screenshots on any servers or cloud storage.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics tools. There is no data sharing with any third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
